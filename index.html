About Me

Driven by curiosity, powered by AI

Hi there! I'm a Software Engineering student at King Fahd University of Petroleum & Minerals (KFUPM) with a 3.86 GPA, currently on the Dean's List. I was the first KFUPM student selected to represent the university as an exchange student at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore.

My passion lies at the intersection of AI and software development. As a SAP Generative AI Developer and Research Assistant at SDAIA-KFUPM Joint Research Center, I work on cutting-edge projects involving LLMs, Computer Vision, and RAG systems. I was also selected among 300 participants from 2,222 applicants for the prestigious KAUST MENA ML Winter School.

Beyond coding, I serve as Vice President of the AI for All (AIFA) Club and have delivered 140+ tutoring hours across 5 courses. I believe in giving back to the community and helping others succeed in their tech journey.
